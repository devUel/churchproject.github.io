About Us

GOOD
SHEPHERD
PARISH CHURCH

Good Shepherd Parish was the first community established by the Society of African Missions (SMA) in the Philippines. It was first officially inaugurated on April 26, 1986 and was dedicated by the late SJaime Cardinal Sin.

READ MORE

MISSION

Thus, rescpecting uniqueness and differences our mission is to promote the Kingdom of God through the proclamation of the Good News to the community , to effect transformation towards excellence, and to commit to the upliftment to the marginalized sectors of society.

VISION

To be an evangelizing community of disciples united in Christian Faith, participating in Kingdom building through formation of truly Christian communities responding to the signs of the times under the able leadership of priests who image the Good Shepherd.
